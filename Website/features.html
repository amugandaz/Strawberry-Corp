How 3DIT works

3DIT is a platform that lets people connect with 3D printer owners in their region, providing a fast, convenient sharing service.

Step 1 - Register

When creating an account you will have to option to indicate that you have a printer that you are willing to register to the service

Step 2 - Manage your settings

3DIT was created with user choice in mind, sellers as well as buyers have a variety of options to their disposal concerning stuff such as:

  • materials
  • range
  • size
  • resolution of print
  • supports
  • delivery

Step 3 - Manage orders

Once everything is set up you can start accepting/sending orders

Step 4 - Enjoy
